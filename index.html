what we do

NeverSleep national Park is home to more than 500 thousands species of Vietnamese animals and Vietnamese native plants, with more than 1,000 of these listed as being at threat of extinction. NSP works to collect data to identify the distribution of threatened species and focus conservation efforts in areas of need.
